About Me

After completing my BS in Community Health and recieving Certified Health Education Specialist, I worked in both direct service and administrative support roles in the non-profit sector. Through the expereinces I started to see how tech was underutilized, and where, when it is innovatively applied, it makes a huge difference in program reach and outcomes.

I have been inspired by the ways tech is being harnessed for community support services. From processing and sharing data, to making services and opportunities more accessible, to developing creative solutions to health and social issues, to helping a commnity feel more connected. I want to be a part of that!
